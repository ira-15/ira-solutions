Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 15/07/2025

Thank you for shopping with Ira Solutions! Here’s what you need to know about our shipping process.

1. Service Areas

We deliver across most PIN codes in India. If your location is not serviceable, we will notify you after your order is placed.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping fees are displayed clearly at checkout. Free shipping may be offered on promotional items or if your order reaches a specific value threshold.

3. Processing Time

Orders are processed and prepared for shipment within 1–2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.

4. Delivery Time

The estimated delivery time is typically 3–7 business days from the date of shipment, though this may vary depending on your specific location and the courier service.

5. Tracking

Tracking details will be sent to your registered email or phone number once the order is shipped from our warehouse.

6. Delays

We are not responsible for delays caused by external courier issues or unforeseen external events (such as extreme weather, strikes, or public service disruptions), but we will actively assist with tracking and resolution efforts.

7. Damaged Deliveries

If your package arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged packaging and the product inside so we can quickly arrange a replacement or refund.
